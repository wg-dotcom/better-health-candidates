About the Role

Driving patient acquisition and revenue growth for a modern medical supply company

better. is a modern medical supplier based in San Francisco, delivering Continuous Glucose Monitors, catheters, ostomy bags, incontinence products, and wound care supplies directly to patients. They partner with leading brands and operate through a Shopify-powered platform with a strong focus on accessibility and patient experience.

This role requires someone who can drive patient acquisition through consultative selling, manage complex account relationships, and convert inbound leads into long-term customers. The ideal candidate combines strong phone sales ability with the empathy and patience needed to serve patients managing chronic health conditions.

What matters most: resilience on the phones, a detail-oriented approach to patient records and compliance, the ability to wear multiple hats, and genuine care for the people on the other end of the line. This is a role where sales meets healthcare, and the right person will thrive in both.
