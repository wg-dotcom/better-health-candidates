About the Role

High-volume inside sales converting warm leads into patients for a modern medical supplier

better. is a healthcare technology company specializing in medical supplies and virtual reality therapy. They deliver Continuous Glucose Monitors, catheters, ostomy bags, incontinence products, and wound care supplies directly to patients across the US. The company is expanding its sales department to manage a surge in leads generated through paid advertising, primarily Meta.

This Inside Sales Agent role is built around high-volume outbound calling: 75 calls per day during the first two weeks, ramping to 135 calls per day plus inbound handling from day 14 onward. The agent will work warm leads, handle live objections, close during the conversation, and support insurance verification. No callbacks or follow-up email selling. Everything happens on the phone, in real time.

What matters most: 2-4 years of phone-based sales experience targeting US consumers, strong live objection handling, clear and neutral spoken English, and the ability to thrive in a dialer or call center environment. This is a direct hiring model focused on reducing turnover and integrating agents into the company's long-term growth.
